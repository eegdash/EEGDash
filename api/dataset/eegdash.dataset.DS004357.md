## About This Dataset

Grootswagers T., Robinson A.K., Shatek S.M., Carlson T.A. (2024). Mapping the Dynamics of Visual Feature Coding: Insights into Perception and Integration. PLoS Computational Biology, 20(1) e1011760 [https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pcbi.1011760](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pcbi.1011760)
Experiment Details
Electroencephalography recordings from 16 subjects to fast streams of gabor-like stimuli.
Images were presented in rapid serial visual presentation streams at 6.67Hz and 20Hz rates. Participants performed an orthogonal fixation colour change detection task.
Experiment length: 1 hour
