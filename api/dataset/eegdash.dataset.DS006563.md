## About This Dataset

**Overview**

This dataset was originally published in
Gramann, K., TГ¶llner, T. and MГјller, H.J. (2010), Dimension-based attention modulates early visual processing. Psychophysiology, 47: 968-978. [https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1469-8986.2010.00998.x](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1469-8986.2010.00998.x)
It was subsequently used to investigate automatic labeling of independent components in ICA and is referred to as the вЂњCueвЂќ dataset:
FrГёlich, L., Andersen, T.S. and MГёrup, M. (2015), Multi-class classification of ICS of EEG. Psychophysiol, 52: 32-45. [https://doi.org/10.1111/psyp.12290](https://doi.org/10.1111/psyp.12290)

ICA was performed with the implementation of the ICA infomax algorithm in the Brain Vision Analyzer software from Brain Products GmbH.2 The data sets we had access to were between 56 and 66вЂ‰min long for the different subjects.вЂќ After contacting the above authors, Laura FrГёlich provided a copy of the data. With Klaus GramannвЂ™s permission, this was converted to BIDS format by Austin J. Brockmeier and Carlos H. Mendoza-Cardenas.
Continuous EEG from 12 with ICA weights.

**Subjects**

вЂњTwelve observers took part in the Experiment (2 female; age range 21вЂ“25 years). All were right-handed, had normal or corrected-to-normal vision, and reported no history of neurological disorder. Observers were either paid or received course credit for participating. All observers provided written informed consent, and the experimental procedure was approved by the ethics committee of the Department of Psychology, University of Munich, in accordance with the Code of Ethics of the World Medical Association (Declaration of Helsinki).вЂќ

**Expert-annotated Independent Components (ICs)**

The expert-annotated labels of the ICs can be found in the field `expert_ica_labels`, and the class names in `ica_classes`. `expert_ica_labels(i)` is the Matlab-index of `ica_classes` for the i-th IC. ICs can be computed using the fields `data`, `icasphere`, and `icaweights` (e.g., `icaact = icaweights * icasphere * data`).
